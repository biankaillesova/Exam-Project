Earl Grey - IceTea with Peaches

Ingredients Procedure

Ingredients

4 teabags of Pickwick Earl Grey
1 ripe peach
2 tablespoons of cane sugar
5 slices of fresh ginger
Lemon juice from half a lemon
Mint leaves for decoration
500 ml of hot water
250 ml of cold soda or water
Ice

Procedure

In 500 ml of hot water, infuse 4 bags of Pickwick Earl Grey black tea together with ginger slices. Remove the black tea after 1-3 minutes, but leave the ginger in the jug. Sweeten to taste and let cool. Then add the peach cut into thin slices and top up with chilled soda. Season with lemon juice, add ice and garnish with mint.
