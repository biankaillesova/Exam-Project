Pineapple & Lemon - Pineapple Punch

Ingredients Procedure

Ingredients

4 bags of Pickwick Pineapple & Lemon tea
2 nails
1 cinnamon roll
1 anise star
peel and juice of 2 lemons
1‚ÅÑ2 diced pineapple
1-2 tablespoons of cane sugar
1 liter of water
condensed milk to taste

Procedure

Grate the peel of 1 lemon. Put it together with the spices in a pot and heat so that the spices ring. Add diced pineapple and pour 1 liter of water. After 10 minutes of cooking, squeeze the pineapple with a fork to release the juice. Add 4 bags of tea and leave to infuse for about 5 minutes. Add lemon juice and cane sugar.
